Our Faculties

Faculty of Applied Arts

The Faculty of Applied Arts is the finest and newest faculty in Tamale Technical University. The faculty was established to manage training, applied research and technological development in the arts, fashion and media environment.

Readmore
Faculty of Applied Science & Technology

This is one of the main faculties of Tamale Technical University. The faculty envisions to provide an environment for excellence in teaching, learning and research in applied sciences and technology.

Readmore
School of Business

The Faculty of Business at TaTU converts students’ potential into career success, with the skills to become leaders in today’s world-wide economy. The Faculty of Business’ degree and Higher National Diploma programs focus on the core disciplines .

Readmore
School of Built Environment

The Faculty of Built and Natural Environment (FBNE) was recently carved out of the then Faculty of Engineering and Built Environment. The FBNE is currently going through initial setups.

Readmore
Faculty of Engineering

This is the oldest faculty in Tamale Technical University. The faculty is envisioned to become a centre of excellence in Ghana and beyond for teaching, learning and research and entrepreneurial training in Engineering, Science and Technology
